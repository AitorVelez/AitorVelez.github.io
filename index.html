About ME

My experience with videogames.

  • 2005-2011

    My first contact with videogames

    When I was a kid, my parents gave me, my first console. It was the Nintendo DS. I was fascinated about it and i couldn't stop playing the games of Mario saga.

  • 2011-2016

    PS4 and Triple A

    While growing up, I continued playing games, in PC, Wii, and in PS4, at these time I started playing Triple A games, and one of them stood out for me, the Uncharted saga. During my high school years I decided that I wanted to be part of those teams that created video games.

  • 2016-2021

    Degree & My devotion to videogames

    In 2016, I enrolled to CITM (UPC), and I started the Game design & development degree. During the degree I've seen some of the main disciplines in videogame creation, but I would be excited if I could join a game development company.

  • 2022-Actual time

    Junior QA Support (iVoox)

    My main responsabilities during this time have been bug reporting and testing. I’ ve also been part of the technical support team, where I helped users with their technical difficulties or their suggestions. We work in an agile environment.
